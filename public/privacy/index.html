Privacy Notice

Last updated July 21, 2026

HiTech Labs respects your privacy. This notice explains what information the website collects, why it is used, and the choices available to you.

Information you provide

When you send a message or request a consultation, you may provide your name, business name, email address, phone number, appointment preferences, service location, and details about the help you need.

Information collected automatically

The hosting service may process basic technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, requested pages, and timestamps for security, reliability, and aggregate website analytics.

How information is used

  • To respond to questions and consultation requests.
  • To schedule and prepare for appointments.
  • To provide requested services and support.
  • To protect the website from spam, abuse, and security threats.
  • To understand aggregate website usage and improve the site.

Service providers

The website is hosted by Cloudflare Pages. Contact and appointment submissions are processed by Formspree. Web fonts are delivered by Google Fonts. These providers process information according to their own privacy terms and only as needed to provide their services.

Sharing and selling

HiTech Labs does not sell or rent personal information. Information may be shared with service providers that support the website or when required by law.

Retention

Inquiry and appointment information is kept only as long as reasonably needed to respond, provide services, maintain business records, and meet applicable legal obligations.

Your choices

You may ask to access, correct, or delete information you submitted by emailing info@hitechlabskauai.com.

Children

This business website is not directed to children under 13, and HiTech Labs does not knowingly collect their personal information.

Updates

This notice may be updated when the website or business practices change. The date above identifies the latest version.
